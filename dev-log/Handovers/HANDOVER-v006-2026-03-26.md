## Decisions Made
- **Card-based dashboard:** Teal card = the product (circle, KFG, actions). White card below = "more stuff" placeholder.
- **Page is neutral:** Off-white (#FAFAFA) background. Cards lift with shadow, not colour wash.
- **Unified shadow:** Both cards use same neutral shadow `rgba(0,0,0,0.08)` — teal tint was too heavy.
- **Footer gone:** Privacy/About/Contact move to hamburger menu. Dashboard is personal space.
- **Explainer panels gone:** They belong on the logged-out home page, not the dashboard.
- **Skillset names synced to Bible:**
  - Curiosity: Question → Challenge → Create → Innovate
  - Collaboration: Engage → Connect → Elevate → Inspire
  - Communication: Update → Navigate → Persuade → Guide
  - Critical Thinking: Clarify → Simplify → Solve → Innovate
