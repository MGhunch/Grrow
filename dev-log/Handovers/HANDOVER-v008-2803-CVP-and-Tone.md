## Decisions Made
- **CVP Learner:** See where they're at. Know where they're aiming. Understand how to grow faster.
- **CVP Leader:** Spot gaps. Coach smarter. Share the right knowledge at the right time.
- **Differentiator:** Better conversations. Less paperwork.
- **Proof:** Focused on core skillsets that everyone needs. Designed around the way people learn. Smart reports, simple tracking. Everything in one place.
- **Tone principle:** Grrow is designed to feel light, but it's not lightweight. Confident in the science. Ruthless about keeping stuff simple.
- **Tone:** Confident but never corporate. Honest but never harsh. Warm and fun without being fluffy or forced.
- **Voice:** Peer mentor — been there, gets it, talks with you not at you
