# HANDOVER v001 — 2026-03-26

## Project
Grrow — a structured professional development tool mapping skills across four strengths and four career stages, helping leaders lead and learners grow.

## Session Focus
Redesigned the SkillsetCard component (the explainer that appears when you click a circle segment). Evolved it from a dense breadcrumb-heavy card to a clean icon-left pattern matching the KFG cards, with a decorative quote mark framing the objective as a question.

## Code Updated
- `src/components/SkillsetCard.tsx` — NEW: clean icon-left card with coloured title, quote mark, "Do you...?" framing
- `src/app/(app)/dashboard/page.tsx` — removed inline SegmentDetail, now uses SkillsetCard

## Decisions Made
- **Icon-left pattern** — matches KFG cards for visual consistency
- **Coloured titles** — purple or teal per family, not black
- **Decorative quote mark** — Georgia serif, light family colour, no closing quote (intentional — it's an invitation, not a finished statement)
- **"Do you...?" framing** — objectives now render as questions: "Do you identify which ideas have the most potential...?"
- **Always show objective** — no hidden/expandable state, too much clickery
- **No extra icons** — they clicked the segment, they know where they are
- **Match KFG styling** — 40% opacity border, family-tinted shadow, same scale
- **Width constrained to 66%** — sits proportionally with the rest of the layout
- **"Cultivate" over "Stay"** — for Curiosity/Question objective (discussed, not yet applied to constants)
